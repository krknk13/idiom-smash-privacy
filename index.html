Privacy Policy

App: Idiom Smash (成語爆擊)

Developer: Sunny Kitten Games

Effective Date: 11 Feb 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sunny Kitten Games (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use Idiom Smash (成語爆擊) (the “App”). The App is an educational puzzle game designed for primary school students.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask for or collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, photos, or contacts.

The App may collect limited non-personal, anonymous information to improve stability and user experience, such as:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Basic app usage events (e.g., app opened, level completed)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

2. Advertising

The App may display advertisements. Ads are provided by third-party partners such as Google AdMob.

If the App is used by children, we aim to show non-personalized ads and avoid behavioral profiling. Rewarded ads are optional and are shown only when a user chooses to watch an ad to receive an in-game reward (e.g., hints).

Please note: third-party ad providers may collect limited device information (such as coarse device signals) to serve ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance, subject to their own policies.

3. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We may use analytics and crash reporting services (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics) to understand app performance and improve gameplay. These services are intended to collect anonymous usage and diagnostic data.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We may share anonymous, non-personal data with service providers only as needed to:

  • Serve advertisements
  • Measure app performance and fix crashes
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Choices

You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the App. If the App includes in-app settings (e.g., vibration), you can adjust them within the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
